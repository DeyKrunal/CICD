# Beta Testing - TaskFlow

Beta Testing is performed **after** Alpha Testing, using a small group of **real, outside users**. The goal is to collect genuine feedback and uncover usability issues the development team might not notice themselves.

## Instructions for Beta Testers

Thank you for helping test TaskFlow! Please:

1. Register a new account and explore the app freely for about 10–15 minutes.
2. Try creating, updating, and deleting a few tasks.
3. Try to "break" things — click quickly, enter unusual input, resize your browser window, etc.
4. Fill out the feedback form below as you go, or right after you finish.
5. Be honest — negative feedback is just as valuable as positive feedback!
